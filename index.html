Permit Categories

Our construction permit checklist is divided into four main categories to help you organize and track all required permits for your project:

Permit and Plancheck Items

Covers architectural approvals, building permits, specialized permits for different construction phases, and various agency planchecks.

View Details
Utility Fees and Assessments

Includes utility connections, easements, impact fees, meter installations, and permits related to public right-of-way.

View Details
Special Approvals and Requirements

Covers environmental permits, community-related fees, specialized approvals, and assessments from various agencies.

View Details
Testing and Inspection

Includes all inspection fees, material testing requirements, commissioning, and specialized inspection services.

Getting Started

Follow these steps to effectively use the permit checklist system for your construction project:

  1. Review all permit categories to identify which ones apply to your project
  2. Use the interactive checklist to assign responsibilities to team members
  3. Add estimated costs and notes for each permit item
  4. Track progress using the project dashboard
  5. Update status as permits are obtained and inspections are completed
